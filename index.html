ABOUT US

Who We Are

Farmington is a specialist chain of retail stores that sell fruits and vegetables. It is the brainchild of Varghese Poulose, a far-sighted visionary backed by 35 years of experience.

Paving the way for a silent revolution of lifestyle & wellness among Keralites, he is launching 50 stores across the state – with one Farmington store every 10 kilometres.

Farmington is a unique one-stop-shop that serves up green groceries in its freshest form possible. For this family-run business, freshness is not just a catchphrase but a daily practice and the very essence of the philosophy we try to emulate.
